FELT & STEEL

A WET HOLE THAT LACKED PASSION
YOUR HAIR IS A SECRET TO BE SHARED
THERE IS METAL WHERE THE MEAT IS MEANT TO BE

This is the day the sky opened up. This is the day you saw the moon from my window, compared it to a hook or maybe a hangnail and fell asleep. This is the day a comet passes unnotice and we miss our chance at another wish. This is the day you wake up in a very large

NEWNESS

YOU ARE SICK BONE BOY
YOU ARE CALLOUS WHERE
I AM SMOOTH, BONE BOY I
